My Beloved Rechelle,

Infinite like the moon's gentle glow,
A circle unbroken, my love will show
What rests in secret, waiting for you,
A treasure as timeless as skies so blue.

Not found in boxes wrapped with care,
But where you keep what's precious and rare,
Between the folds of memories kept,
Or where your most loved items are swept.

It winks at dawn, it shines at night,
A band of promise, pure and bright,
When found, you'll know what it conveys -
My heart is yours in countless ways.

Search not far, but look nearby,
The answer's hidden in plain sight,
And if the clues don't make it clear,
Tomorrow's dawn will bring it near.

Happy birthday, my clever sweet,
Solve this riddle - no easy feat!
But know whatever you may do,
My greatest gift is loving you.

Forever yours,
